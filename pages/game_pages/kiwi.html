Kiwi

Developed by TEAM APEX

Kiwi is a three level auto-scroller that focuses on the endless adventure during which the player must help maneuver the bird in order to dodge environmental obstacles whilst trying to not get killed by enemies! The powerup store gives the player an opportunity to utilize the coins collected throughout the game as well as a greater chance to get higher scores on the leaderboard. Players can also get information regarding the featured endangered species they will encounter throughout the game! This current beta version of the game includes three levels, all in New Zealand.

Level 1 - Poacher's Forest

Welcome to the Poacher's Forest! This is the first level that Kiwi has to face in order to find his parents. Kiwi is able to land on stone pillars to recover his flight energy. However, be mindful of the vines and flying ferrets. Try throwing a kiwi at 'em to take them down! The gusts of wind will help Kiwi fy more easily.

Level 2 - Auckland Airways

This is Auckland Airways. In here, Kiwi is able to use planes to land on. There will be clouds that you can bounce on! You will encounter hedgehogs on this level, but do not be fooled by their cuteness, these little guys are ready to take Kiwi down and they will shoot their quills at him to do so. Your kiwi fruit will destroy their quills, use that to your advantage! Some of these hedgehogs will also chase you! Are you up for the challenge?

Level 3 - Ceniza's Island

This is the last level in Kiwi's journey. Help Kiwi fly through a volcano island. You will face rising magma waves that you will need to avoid, while also dodging the falling meteors. The meteors will chase you, so prepare your kiwi fruit to knock them out. You will get the chance to stop the falling meteors by shooting your kiwi fruit at the active volcano. But, you must get the kiwi right inside of the volcano or it will not work! Think you you can take the heat and reunite Kiwi with his parents?
